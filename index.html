About

Hey, thanks for stopping by.

I am currently working at Google. I got my PhD at Carnegie Mellon University, advised by Gabriela Hug and J. Zico Kolter. I got my BS from Electrical Engineering, Tsinghua with honors - Best Graduate of Beijing (Tsinghua).

How to pronounce "Xiao"? Its pronunciation is very similar to "Shaw", so just call me "Shaw". ( Pronunciation on Youtube: Xiao Zhang )
