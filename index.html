About

Hey, thanks for stopping by.

I am currently a PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University, working on interesting interdisciplinary research projects, advised by Gabriela Hug and J. Zico Kolter. My research interests include optimization, algorithms, and their applications in power system and process control. I got my BS from Electrical Engineering, Tsinghua with honors (Best Graduate of Beijing and of Tsinghua). I am also serving as the chief secretary for the Tsinghua Alumni Association of Greater Pittsburgh Area.
