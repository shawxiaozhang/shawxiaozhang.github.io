I am currently a PhD student at Carnegie Mellon University, working on interesting interdisciplinary research projects, advised by Gabriela Hug and J. Zico Kolter. I got my BS from Electrical Engineering, Tsinghua with honors (Best Graduate of Beijing and of Tsinghua). I am also serving as the chief secretary for the Tsinghua Alumni Association of Greater Pittsburgh Area.
